# Topic Backlog — Facts / Kinetic Typography

A researched bank of **surprising-but-true** facts to script. Each is chosen for a strong frame-1 hook + a loop-able payoff. Pull from the top, move it to [VIDEO_LOG.md](VIDEO_LOG.md) once scripted.

> ⚠️ **Always fact-check before publishing.** The viral-facts internet is full of false "facts" (e.g. "we only use 10% of our brain" — false). Each item below is true to current knowledge, but verify the specific numbers against a primary source (encyclopedia, scientific body) before rendering. A debunked fact tanks trust and retention.

## 🧠 Human body & brain
1. **Octopus aside — your stomach lining replaces itself every few days** so it doesn't digest itself. *Hook: "Your stomach would eat itself — so it rebuilds every 3 days."*
2. **You're ~1cm taller in the morning** than at night (spinal discs compress). *Hook: "You shrink every single day."*
3. **Your body has more bacterial cells than human cells** (roughly comparable, ~1.3:1). *Hook: "You're more bacteria than human."* (Verify ratio — older "10:1" claim was debunked.)
4. **Bones are stronger than steel by weight** (but lighter). *Hook: "Your bones beat steel."*
5. **The cornea gets oxygen directly from the air**, not blood. *Hook: "One part of you breathes without blood."*

## 🐙 Nature & animals
6. **Octopuses have 3 hearts and blue blood.** *Hook: "An octopus has 3 hearts."*
7. **Honey never spoils** — 3,000-year-old edible honey found in tombs. *Hook: "This food never expires."*
8. **Bananas are berries; strawberries are not.** *Hook: "A banana is a berry. A strawberry isn't."*
9. **Wombat poop is cube-shaped.** *Hook: "This animal poops cubes."*
10. **Sharks are older than trees** (~400M vs ~350M years). *Hook: "Sharks existed before trees."*
11. **A group of flamingos is called a 'flamboyance.'** *Hook: "The most extra animal group name."*
12. **Sea otters hold hands while sleeping** so they don't drift apart. *Hook: "Otters hold hands so they don't lose each other."*

## 🌌 Space
13. **A day on Venus is longer than its year.** *Hook: "One planet's day is longer than its year."*
14. **It rains diamonds on Neptune and Uranus.** *Hook: "It literally rains diamonds here."*
15. **There are more stars than grains of sand on every beach on Earth.** *Hook: "Count every grain of sand. There are more stars."*
16. **Neutron star material: a teaspoon weighs ~billions of tons.** *Hook: "A teaspoon of this weighs a billion tons."*
17. **You could fit every planet in the solar system between Earth and the Moon.** *Hook: "Every planet fits in this gap."* (Verify with mean distances.)

## 📜 History
18. **Oxford University is older than the Aztec Empire.** *Hook: "This university predates the Aztecs."*
19. **Cleopatra lived closer to the Moon landing than to the building of the Great Pyramid.** *Hook: "Cleopatra is closer to us than to the pyramids."* ← scripted as Episode F-001
20. **The fax machine was invented before the telephone.** *Hook: "The fax is older than the phone."*
21. **Nintendo was founded in 1889** (made playing cards). *Hook: "Nintendo is older than the lightbulb's spread."* (Verify framing.)
22. **The last execution by guillotine in France was 1977** — same year Star Wars released. *Hook: "France used the guillotine the year Star Wars came out."*

## 🔢 Math & mind-benders
23. **The birthday paradox: in a room of 23 people, ~50% chance two share a birthday.** *Hook: "23 people. 50% two share a birthday."*
24. **A piece of paper folded 42 times would reach the Moon.** *Hook: "Fold paper 42 times → you hit the Moon."*
25. **There are more possible chess games than atoms in the observable universe.** *Hook: "Chess has more games than the universe has atoms."*

## 🍔 Food & everyday
26. **Carrots were originally purple**, not orange. *Hook: "Carrots used to be purple."*
27. **Peanuts are not nuts** — they're legumes. *Hook: "A peanut isn't a nut."*
28. **Ketchup was once sold as medicine** (1830s). *Hook: "Ketchup used to be medicine."*
29. **The 'wood' in a pencil writes with graphite, never lead.** *Hook: "Pencils have never had lead."*
30. **Hot water can freeze faster than cold** (Mpemba effect — conditional/debated). *Hook: "Hot water can freeze faster than cold."* (⚠️ Caveat: effect is real but conditional — frame carefully.)

When this list runs low, refill it by:
- Picking a sub-theme that's retaining well in Analytics and going deeper on it.
- Cross-checking each candidate against a primary source before it enters this file.
- Watching which facts get the most rewatches/comments and spinning sequels ("Part 2: more space facts").

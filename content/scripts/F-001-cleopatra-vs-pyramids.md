> Every on-screen claim traces to `01-verified-facts.md` (claim id in brackets). No new facts. Numbers shown rounded; exact dates are the support beats. **Cleopatra's death day is NOT shown** (disputed) — only years.

## 📌 Overview
- **Working title:** Cleopatra is closer to us than to the Pyramids
- **Format:** Kinetic typography (timeline / number reveal)
- **Niche:** Facts / Kinetic Typography
- **Total runtime:** 28 s  (= **840 frames** @ 30fps)
- **One-line premise:** Cleopatra lived ~2,500 years after the Great Pyramid but only ~2,000 years before the Moon landing — so she's closer in time to *us* than to the monuments she lived beside.

## 🎯 Hook Frame — 0.0s to 1.5s (frames 0–45)
> This is the thumbnail. Legible in <0.5s, promises the payoff.

- **On-screen text:** "Cleopatra is closer to **YOU**" (line 1, big) / "than to the **Pyramids**" (line 2, big)
- **Visual:** Pure type on a deep night-sky background. A faint timeline rule runs horizontally across center. Tiny pyramid glyph hard-left, tiny Moon/rocket glyph hard-right, the word "Cleopatra" pinned just right of center — visually closer to the Moon side. "YOU" punches in scaled with a quick overshoot.
- **Why it stops the swipe:** Counterintuitive bold claim — two "ancient Egypt" icons most people assume are neighbors in time, collapsed by a "wait, what?" gap. [C8]

## 🎬 Beats (~3–5s each — one cut per beat)

### Beat 1 — 1.5s–5.0s (frames 45–150)
- **On-screen text:** "The Great Pyramid —" → "built **~2560 BC**" (the year scales/locks in)
- **Visual / animation:** Pyramid glyph draws in at far LEFT of the timeline; label "2560 BC" snaps beneath it with a stone-impact shake. Background star field drifts slowly.
- **Source:** [C1] [C2] [C3]

### Beat 2 — 5.0s–8.5s (frames 150–255)
- **On-screen text:** "Cleopatra —" → "born **69 BC**"
- **Visual / animation:** "Cleopatra" label slides in and lands far to the RIGHT of the pyramid on the timeline; "69 BC" counts/locks beneath it. A measuring bracket begins stretching from the pyramid toward her.
- **Source:** [C4] [C5] [C6]

### Beat 3 — 8.5s–12.5s (frames 255–375)
- **On-screen text:** "That's **~2,500 years** apart" (the number is the hero)
- **Visual / animation:** The bracket from Pyramid→Cleopatra fills and a counter ticks **0 → 2,500** (years) along it. Big "~2,500 YEARS" stamps center. Beat lands on the full gap.
- **Source:** derived from [C1]+[C4] (2560 − 69 ≈ 2,491 → ~2,500)

### Beat 4 — 12.5s–16.0s (frames 375–480)
- **On-screen text:** "The Moon landing?" → "**1969**"
- **Visual / animation:** Camera/timeline pans RIGHT past Cleopatra to a Moon + rocket glyph at the far right; "1969" locks in with a small launch-flash. Cleopatra label stays visible mid-frame for reference.
- **Source:** [C7]

### Beat 5 — 16.0s–20.0s (frames 480–600)
- **On-screen text:** "Only **~2,000 years** after her"
- **Visual / animation:** A *second* bracket stretches Cleopatra→Moon; counter ticks **0 → 2,000**. Place it directly under the first bracket so the two lengths are visually comparable — the second is clearly shorter.
- **Source:** derived from [C4]+[C7] (69 + 1969 = 2,038 → ~2,000)

### Beat 6 — 20.0s–25.5s (frames 600–765)  ← PAYOFF
- **On-screen text:** "She's **~450 years closer**" → "to the **Moon landing**" → "than to the Pyramids"
- **Visual / animation:** Both brackets sit stacked; the shorter (Moon side) pulses/glows. The two numbers 2,500 vs 2,000 flash, then "~450 YEARS CLOSER" stamps over the Cleopatra label, which nudges visibly toward the Moon side. Quiet beat to let it land.
- **Source:** [C8] + derived (2,491 − 2,038 ≈ 450)

## 🔁 Loop-Back — final ~1.5s (frames 765–840)
> Make the auto-loop invisible so replays compound reach.

- **Final frame composition:** Everything dissolves back to the exact Hook Frame layout — same night-sky bg, same timeline rule, "Cleopatra is closer to **YOU** than to the **Pyramids**" in the identical position/scale as frame 0.
- **The loop trick:** The hook is a claim; Beats 1–6 are the proof.

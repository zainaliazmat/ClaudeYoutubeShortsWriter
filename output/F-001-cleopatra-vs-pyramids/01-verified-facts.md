## 🔢 The math (derived from verified dates above — this IS the payoff)

- **Pyramid completed:** ~2560 BC (C1, C3)
- **Cleopatra born:** 69 BC (C4)  ·  **died:** 30 BC (C5)
- **Moon landing:** AD 1969 (C7)

| Gap | Calculation | Result |
|-----|-------------|--------|
| Pyramid → Cleopatra (birth) | 2560 − 69 | **≈ 2,491 years** (round: ~2,500) |
| Cleopatra (birth) → Moon landing | 69 + 1969 | **= 2,038 years** (round: ~2,000) |
| **Difference** | 2,491 − 2,038 | **Cleopatra is ~450 yrs CLOSER to the Moon landing** |

> Sanity check with death date: Cleopatra (30 BC) → Moon landing = 30 + 1969 = **1,999 yrs**; Pyramid → Cleopatra death = 2560 − 30 = **2,530 yrs**. Conclusion holds either way. Script should use **birth (69 BC)** for the cleanest "~2,500 vs ~2,000" framing.

## ⚠️ Refuted / dropped — DO NOT use in script

- ❌ "Pyramid completed ~2580 BC" — 2580 BC is the **start** of construction; completion is ~2560 BC. (multiple high-quality sources)
- ❌ "Pyramid completed 2504 BC / built in 85 years (2589–2504 BC)" — that 85-year span covers **all three** Giza pyramids, not the Great Pyramid alone; conclusion misread from aeraweb.org.
- ❌ Cleopatra died "August 10" **or** "August 12" 30 BC as a precise day — the exact **day** is genuinely disputed/unrecorded. Use only the **year 30 BC**; do not state a calendar day on screen.
- ❌ "Ptolemy XII willed Cleopatra the kingdom (sole ruler)" — she **co-ruled** with Ptolemy XIII; don't imply sole inheritance.

## Notes on framing safety
- Pyramid dating carries scholarly uncertainty (~2580–2540 BC window), but the ~2,500-yr vs ~2,000-yr comparison is robust across the entire range — the fact does not depend on the exact year. Safe to present "~2,500 years" / "~2,000 years" as approximate.
- Use round numbers on screen ("~2,500", "~2,000", "~450 years closer"); cite exact dates (2560 BC, 69 BC, 1969) as the supporting beats.

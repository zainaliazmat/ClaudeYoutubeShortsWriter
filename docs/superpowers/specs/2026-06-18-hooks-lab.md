Scroll-stopper scoring (0-5): **dissonance** (does it create instant cognitive
contradiction), **clarity** (understood in <0.5s on a phone), **specificity** (a concrete
number/anchor), **curiosity gap** (does it withhold a payoff). Higher = stronger.

## Recommendation

Lead both videos with the **"YOU"** variant (hook-f001-you, hook-f002-you): highest
dissonance + clarity, personal, and the parallel framing gives the channel a recognizable
repeatable device (the brand-spine point both CEO voices raised). Keep the **number**
variant as the A/B challenger (it scores highest on specificity). The **moon/compare**
framings are better deployed as mid-video beats than as the cold-open thumbnail.
